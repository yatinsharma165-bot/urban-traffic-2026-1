The Problem & Solution

Why Smart Traffic Management Matters

Urban populations are growing rapidly, but road infrastructure can't expand fast enough. Traditional traffic signals operate on fixed timers — they can't react to actual traffic conditions.

Our AI-powered system collects real-time data on vehicle count, speed, and density at each intersection. It then dynamically adjusts green signal duration to reduce waiting time, congestion, and fuel waste.
